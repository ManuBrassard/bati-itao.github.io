Accessibility Awareness

The IT Accessibility Office provides accessibility awareness and information services. On this page, you will find a list of awareness days, trainings, events and articles related to accessibility.

Articles

It is a small initiative to bring awareness through articles focusing on the importance of various Awareness Days pertaining to Accessibility and their issues. Articles includes upcoming events, trainings and more.

Events

The following are awareness events offered by the IT Accessibility Office. You will have the opportunity to deepen your knowledge of the community with various disabilities as well as content related to accessibility.

Training and Coaching

The purpose of the training is to develop and create accessible documents and applications as well as deepen our knowledge of adaptive technological methods used by people with functional limitations.
