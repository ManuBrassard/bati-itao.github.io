Training and Coaching 2023

The purpose of the training is to develop and create accessible documents and applications as well as deepen our knowledge of adaptive technological methods used by people with functional limitations.

November

The IT Accessibility Office (ITAO) is pleased to offer the following courses, these courses are offered in both French & English. Please refer to the details below to register:

  • Creating Accessible Excel Documents using Microsoft 365 Internal (Course ID: 0000134741)

    Learn best practices when using Excel Microsoft 365 to create accessible content. Basic knowledge of Excel is required.

    English Session: November 7 | 10:00 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. (ET)
    French Session: November 9 | 10:00 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. (ET)
  • Information and Communication Technologies Accessibility for Web Developers. Part 1 and 2 Internal (Course ID: 0000124668)

    Learn the basics on how to create accessible Information and Communication Technologies using the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1. Knowledge of HTML programming and functionalities is highly recommended. No previous accessibility knowledge is required.

    English Session Part 1: November 21 | 12:30 p.m. to 4:30 p.m. (ET)
    English Session Part 2: November 28 | 12:30 p.m. to 4:30 p.m. (ET)
  • Introduction to Digital Accessibility Internal (Course ID: 0000134733)

    Learn about different types of barriers that the digital environment may create for people with different abilities and why inclusive design eliminates them. This training contains concepts and not technical subjects.

    English Session: November 29 | 1:30 p.m. to 2:30 p.m. (ET)
    French Session: November 29 | 10:30 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. (ET)
