Training and Coaching 2023

The purpose of the training is to develop and create accessible documents and applications as well as deepen our knowledge of adaptive technological methods used by people with functional limitations.

Did you know that many other learning opportunities are offered by the College@ESDC? Use the Course Finder tool Internal on iService to find those best suited for you! In the Course Finder subject field, select 'Diversity, Accessibility and inclusion'. For more information or for accessible methods of registration please contact the College@ESDC: nc-college-gd@servicecanada.gc.ca.

To Register for the class of your choice or to complete the training, participants must Sign in to their Saba account. New to SABA account?

Don't have a Saba account? No problem! - just follow Instructions to access SABA account Internal and register to the events.

July

The IT Accessibility Office (ITAO) is pleased to offer the following courses, these courses are offered in both French & English. Please refer to the details below to register:

  • Creating an Accessible Excel Document with M365 Internal (Course ID: 0000134741)

    Learn best practices when using Excel Microsoft 365 to create accessible content. Basic knowledge of Excel is required.

    English Session: July 6 | 1:30 PM to 3:00 PM (Eastern Standard Time)
  • Introduction to Digital Accessibility Internal (Course ID: 0000134733)

    Learn about different types of barriers that the digital environment may create for people with different abilities and why inclusive design eliminates them. This training contains concepts and not technical subjects.

    English Session: July 11 | 1:30 PM to 2:30 PM (Eastern Standard Time)
  • Create Accessible Content with M365 Internal (Course ID: 0000134734)

    Learn best practices when using Microsoft 365 to create accessible content with Outlook, Word, and PowerPoint.

    English Session: July 12 | 1:00 PM to 4:00 PM (Eastern Standard Time)
    French Session: July 13 | 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM

For more information, questions, or to submit a request please contact IT Accessibility Office edsc.ti-it.a11y.esdc@hrsdc-rhdcc.gc.ca
