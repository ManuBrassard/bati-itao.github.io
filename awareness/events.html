Events

The IT Accessibility Office - Awareness Team provides accessibility awareness and information services. In this section, you will find a list of Upcoming and Past Events. These one-hour virtual events are open to all public servants and delivered via SABA platform or by MS Teams in both official languages.

Upcoming Events

All events are carefully chosen and planned based on awareness days and months and provide the opportunity to deepen your knowledge on various disability topics.

Past Events

If you missed or did not get a chance to join an event, this section will provide you with access to all past event recordings.


Target Audience

  • All ESDC Employees.
  • Anyone involved in developing organization/ department policies (including managers, leaders, directors, etc.;).

If you have any questions or need clarifications, please reach out to the Accessibility Awareness Team by email: EDSC.TI.SENSIBILISATION-AWARENESS.IT.ESDC@hrsdc-rhdcc.gc.ca.
