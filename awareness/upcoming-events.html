October

Living and Working with a Disability Such as Spina Bifida and/or Hydrocephalus

Join the IT Accessibility Office and André Demers, Senior Advisor within the IT Accessibility Office, and learn about this type of disability. André Demers was born with Spina Bifida and Hydrocephalus external, the myelomeningocele type. He will be sharing his story as a person living with a disability and speaking about the challenges, obstacles and different ways that helped him integrate the workplace along the years.
