Upcoming Events

The following are awareness events offered by the IT Accessibility Office. You will have the opportunity to deepen your knowledge of content related to accessibility.

Please note that these sessions will be recorded and available following the week of the event.

To access the previous events, all recordings are available through our Library.

If you require any assistance, please reach out to the Awareness Team by email: EDSC.TI.SENSIBILISATION-AWARENESS.IT.ESDC@hrsdc-rhdcc.gc.ca, no later then a week prior to the event.

Don't have a Saba account? No problem! - just follow Instructions to access SABA account Internal and register to the events.
