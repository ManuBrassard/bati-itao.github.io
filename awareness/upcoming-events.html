The Story of a Young Professional: Journey, Healing, And Lessons Learned

On Global Accessibility Awareness Day, Audrey Camonfour-JobinInternal (Environment and Climate Change Canada) will be speaking about her mental health journey as a young professional. Her objective is to raise awareness and reduce the stigma surrounding mental health in the workplace by talking about her real-life struggles and the recovery process. Additionally, she will discuss how battling with performance anxiety allowed her to develop her own toolkit to better support her well-being.
