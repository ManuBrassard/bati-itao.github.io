Redesigning Life- A Cancer Survivor's Journey to Leadership

The IT Accessibility Awareness Team is hosting a virtual event Redesigning Life- A Cancer Survivor's Journey to Leadership on Thursday, Feb 1, 2024.

Martin Paquette, a cancer survivor from Public Service Procurement Canada (PSPC), is going to share his extraordinary journey! Not only did he triumph over his illness, but he also redesigned his life and eventually achieved the milestone of becoming a manager!
