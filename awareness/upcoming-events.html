Employment and Social Development Canada - IT Accessibility Office Celebrates International Day of Persons with Disabilities

Digital Accessibility is not Just a Good Practice, it’s the Law!

The Accessible Canada Act aims to make Canada a barrier-free nation by January 1, 2040. To contribute to this goal, ESDC’s IT Accessibility Office (ITAO) is actively transforming the digital landscape to ensure our digital products and services are inclusive to all.

In recognition of these efforts, we invite you to join us on December 4, 2024, to commemorate the International Day of Person with Disabilities. Our speaker Luna Bengio, Senior Accessibility Consultant and Strategic Advisor, Shared Services Canada will share her insights on the importance of digital accessibility and the journey towards an inclusive Canada.

Please check with Saba for more information on what you can expect from this session.
