Creating an Accessible Environment for Colour Blind Individuals

Equip your team with the necessary knowledge, skills, and tools to create inclusive digital experiences for all.

Join us for an event on Colour Blind Awareness Day. The IT Accessibility Office’s (ITAO) Accessibility Centre of Excellence (ACE) and the Information and Communication Technology Accessibility Audit (ICTAA) Team will be demonstrating practical techniques and resources to create an accessible environment for colour blind individuals.
